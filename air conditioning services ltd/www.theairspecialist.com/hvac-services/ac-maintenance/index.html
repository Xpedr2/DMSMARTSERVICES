AC Maintenance Services We Offer Throughout Pinellas & Manatee County

For each year your air conditioning system is not regularly maintained, it loses nearly 5% of its original efficiency. This means the longer your system is ignored, the more problems it may encounter and the more money you may pay to run it. The best way to avoid the issues and save money on your energy bill is to have routine maintenance performed.

Our team offers a variety of maintenance services for your convenience, including:

  • Cleaning the equipment
  • Inspecting the unit for proper refrigerant levels
  • Cleaning debris from inside the unit
  • Checking to see if you need to change your air filter
  • Inspecting the compressor and associated tubing for damage
  • Inspecting the coils and cabinets
  • Looking for any leaks around the unit or inside your home

How AC Maintenance Saves You Money

Learn how AC maintenance is geared to saving you money below.

  • Decrease your utility bills – When an AC unit isn't operating at 100% efficiency, it requires more electricity to keep the environment cool. Air conditioners are meant to cool for a period and then rest, but if your AC is lacking in efficiency it might not be resting as much as it should, costing you money every month.
  • Save on AC repairs – When you schedule routine AC maintenance in Sarasota, technicians will inspect, identity, and resolve minor problems with your unit before they get worse and require repair. Since preventative measures are generally less time-consuming, you save money on both parts and labor.
  • Increase your system's lifespan – If you fail to schedule annual maintenance, you'll likely face premature AC replacement, costing you more in the long run. For example, if your AC unit lasts 15 years instead of 10, then over a 30 years period you'll only need to replace your AC once instead of twice.
  • Plan for predictable costs – Another benefit of AC maintenance is that it's a predictable expense for your budget compared to unexpected and potentially costlier repairs. By making a small investment in annual air conditioner inspection and tune-up helps keep your budget organized and your wallet full.
